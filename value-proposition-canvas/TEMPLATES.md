## Fit assessment (the honest part)

- **Fit claim:** our top relievers/creators map to the customer's top-ranked pains/gains — ⟨true on paper / supported by early market response / proven by repeat payment⟩
- **Current fit level:** problem–solution (paper) / early market / product–market → **⟨level⟩**
- **Failure-mode check:** ⬜ feature-first fit (every feature magically maps to a "top" pain)? ⬜ pain inflation without evidence? ⬜ everything "important", nothing ranked?

## MVP (smallest test of the riskiest fit claim)

- Claim under test: ⟨top pain ↔ top reliever⟩
- Form: ⟨concierge/manual service · landing page · prototype⟩ — manual delivery of the real service is often the strongest evidence per build-hour
- Pass/fail signal: ⟨numeric⟩ · Deliberately NOT building: ⟨list⟩

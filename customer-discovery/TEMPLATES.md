# Templates — Customer Discovery

## 1. Interview prep sheet (before any conversation)

```markdown
## Interview prep — ⟨archetype⟩, ⟨date⟩
WHO: ⟨archetype + where found + warm intro?⟩  B2B: user / buyer / saboteur — which one is this?
HYPOTHESES UNDER TEST (3–5 from the F/A/U list):
- A⟨n⟩: ⟨assumption⟩ → listening for: ⟨pass signal⟩ / ⟨fail signal⟩
DUAL-ROLE? ⟨if this is also a sales/service visit: do the job first, learn second,

```

## 2. Question guide skeleton (a guide, not a script — behavior first, open-ended, past tense)

1. **Warm-up:** "How long have you been ⟨doing the job⟩?"
2. **Channel/trigger (if they found you):** "Where did you hear about ⟨it⟩? When? And what was happening *that day* that made you act?"
3. **Status quo:** "These days, how do you ⟨get the job done⟩? How's that going?"
4. **Lost customer (gold):** "You used to ⟨use us/X⟩ — honestly, what made that stop? We genuinely won't mind."
5. **Last-time story (the core):** "Tell me about the LAST time you ⟨did the job⟩ — start to finish. Steps, trips, who was involved, what it cost."
6. **Friction + hacks:** "What was the most annoying part? Ever found a workaround?" *(workarounds = strong evidence of real need)*
7. **Decider/payer:** "Who decides it's time, and who pays?"
8. **Price (behavior-anchored):** "What did you pay last time, all-in? For ⟨your alternative⟩, what would feel fair?" *(never "would you pay X?")*
9. **Channel preference:** "You ⟨called/messaged/walked in⟩ — was that easier than ⟨other channel⟩? How do you usually ⟨book similar services⟩?"
10. **Referrals:** "Who else do you know who ⟨has this job⟩? Could you introduce us?"
11. **Blank's closer:** "What should I have asked you that I didn't?"

Localize phrasing to the customer's actual language/dialect.

## 3. Commitment-signal checklist (fill right after)

- [ ] **Money** — paid, pre-ordered, or bought more than planned, without haggling it away
- [ ] **Time** — gave extra time willingly; asked how to come back
- [ ] **Intros** — actual names/numbers given (not "sure, I'll tell people")
- [ ] **Repeat intent with specifics** — named a concrete upcoming occasion/date
- ⚠️ Enthusiasm with none of the above = politeness, not validation.

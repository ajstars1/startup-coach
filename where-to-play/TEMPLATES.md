## Worksheet 3 — Agile Focus

**Primary opportunity:** O⟨n⟩ — ⟨application + customer⟩ — because ⟨…⟩

| Other opportunity | Product relatedness (tech/resources/networks) | Market relatedness (values/channels/word-of-mouth) | Role | Decision |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| O⟨m⟩ | low/med/high | low/med/high | Growth / Backup | Pursue now / Keep open / Storage |

Rules: keep ≥1 Backup (doesn't share the primary's major risks) and ≥1 Growth open.

## Close with

# Templates — Startup Scorecard

**Scoring bands:** 0 = no evidence · 1 = very weak · 2 = weak · 3 = some evidence · 4 = strong evidence · 5 = multiple strong evidence points.
**Rules:** nothing written down = 0 regardless of confidence · dimension backed only by ASSUMPTIONS caps at 1 · n=1 anecdote caps at 2.

*"Yet to validate — 0" is a useful row, not a shameful one.*
